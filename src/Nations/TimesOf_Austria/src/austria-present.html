Situation in Austria

Austria is politically stable and ensures a high quality of life for its residents. The economy in Austria survived the Corona crisis well compared to other countries and many jobs were saved. The government forms the ÖVP with the Greens, Karl Nehammer replaced Sebastian Kurz after the media published suspicions about Kurz.

Situation outside Austria

Austria plays a rather small role worldwide, and if so, then either as a holiday destination, for its ski team or for its economy. Due to the longstanding partnership with Russian oligarchs, Austria is more affected by the current Ukraine-Russian war than other EU countries. The Austrian Armed Forces have been run down for years and are therefore not operational.
