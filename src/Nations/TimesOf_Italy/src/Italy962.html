The Carolingians

Charlemagne conquered northern Italy for the Franks in 773 against the Lombards. The Carolingian Dynasty ruled over norther Italy for next 114 years, when Charlemagne’s descendant was deposed. For a long time, the throne should be contested by multiple aristocratic families from both within and outside the kingdom.

Emperor of the Romans

In 961, the king of East Francia, Otto I., married Adelaide of Italy, which granted him, how the name tells, the throne of Italy. But to be sure, he invaded Italy anyway and had himself crowned emperor in 962. The union between Italy and Germany was established and Otto was now the first „Emperor of the Romans“.
