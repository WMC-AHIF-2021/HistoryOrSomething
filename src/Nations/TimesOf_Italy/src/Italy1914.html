A united italian nation

The rise of nationalism resulted in the Unification of Germany, but Italy as well. The last century saw the creation of the Kingdom of Italy and its rise to a major player in europe. It participated in the coalition during the Boxer Rebellion in China, got land in Africa after the Kongo-Conference and expanded its influence in trade. However, Italy had some problems. In comparison to Britain or France, Italy was short on military and economic ressources. Italy attempted to make Ethiopia a colony but failed multiple times, due to misunderstandings with the Treaty of Wuchale (1889). This escalated into a war with the Ethiopian Empire, which Italy lost.

Further distabilization came with the italian invasion of today Libya, which was controlled by the Ottomans at the time. Despite Italy gaining the territory as new colony, Italy’s inner conflicts saw the end of Italy’s „liberal era“.
