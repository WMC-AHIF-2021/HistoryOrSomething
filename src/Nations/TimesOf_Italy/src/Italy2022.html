General Information

Today Italy is known for many tourist attractions such as Venice, Napels and Rome, what makes Italy the fifth most visited country behind China. It’s GDP is around 2 trillion with steady growth, although, Italy, too, had a setback when the pandemic hit. Italy has a very modest army, as well, as it the 11th strongest country by PwrIndex. One problem, Italy already is confronted with is the difference between the strong economical and developed North and the agrocultural South. Italy is part of NATO and the EU.

Important to Mention

In the pandemic Italy experienced a heavy economic downturn. Two years later, the economy finally experiences some growth, but a new problem emerges. The war in Ukraine strains all of europe’s economy and this also includes Italy. What the industry fears most is that there soon might be no gas or energy from Russia. This would have fatal consequences for the already run-down economy.
