A divided nation

After Germany lost the Second World War, the german territories were being occupied by the winner forces. The US, UK, France and the Ussr each got a chunk of Germany, with the Ussr and the Us having the biggest part. At the beginning they planned to make Germany a single state again, however, with the beginning of the cold war, the soviets didn't leave the eastern part of Germany and this ended in the creation of two german nations, the FRG and the GDR.

Two opposing brothers

The GDR was a socialist one-party state and a puppet of the big Ussr. Its economy is fully state-owned and the people are under strict control. Meanwhile, in Western Germany, things start to get a lot better. The "Wirtschaftswunder" gave the people new hope and wealth. GDP increased and soon after the FRG was far more ahead economically than its eastern brother.
