Where are the Romans?

After the fall of western Rome, the Franks rose in the area of future France and Germany. Their Leader, Charlemagne, was crowned Emperor.

Now, both the Francs and the Byzantinians claim to be the rightful successor of the Roman Empire.

The Carolingian Empire itself is at its territorial peak, and Charlemagne, of course, wants to ensure its safety. However, since 810 he suffers from illness and because of that, he has to pass on the throne.

His son Ludwig shall run the country.
