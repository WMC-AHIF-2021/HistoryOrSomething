The Empire of Japan

During the Second Sino-Japanese War, Japan managed to conquer most of today's China and Oceania. It stretched from the most northern point of Manchuria to the islands of today Indonesia.

However, due to their aggressive expansion, they withdrew from the League of Nations. And so they allied themselves with Germany and Italy, forming the Tripartite Pact in 1940.

On December 7th 1941, Japan attacked the American naval base in Pearl Harbor. The reason for this was to thread the Americans not to intervene in their military actions in south asia.
The day after, they declared war on the US and the UK, making Germany declaring war on them, as well.
