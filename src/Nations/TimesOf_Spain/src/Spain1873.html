1st Spanish Republic - 1873

Info Table
Capital Madrid
President Estanislao Figueras
Type of Government Presidential republic
Population 16.4 million
Land Area 505,990 km²
Currency Peseta
Democracy doesnt always win

The King of Spain was not much liked by the population, because he was a foreign monarch. One thing leads after another, and he has to abdicate, followed by the proclamation of a state without a king, a republic.

The Republic faces many problems like a war in cuba. Making matters worse, that Spain also has industrial and agricultural problems. In just 1 year, Spain has 4 presidents. But the biggest danger for the republic is simply the lack of support.

Just one year later, in 1874, Alfonso XII is crowned King, ending the 1st Republic of Spain.
