Overall

Germany is one of the major powers in Europe. Fortunately for them, the Industrial Revolution just has occurred, and the population boomed, which made Germany also one of the leading world powers next to Britain, France, Russia and the US. They also possessed the second largest army in Europe with around 3.8 million active personnel behind Russia with over 10 million, which however had a much worse equipped and trained army.

Hot news

After the unification Otto von Bismarck set Germany’s goal to isolate France, its main rival. To achieve this, they needed to maintain good relations with Russia and Austria-Hungary (League of the three Emperors). However, as the Wilhelm II. came to power, Bismarck was removed from his position and the new Emperor set Germany on a different course, a more aggressive and expansionist course. They began to build more ships which escalated into the Anglo-German naval arms race from 1898 to 1912. Also, the League of 3 Emperors began to crumble as Austria and Russia fought for influence in the Balkans and as Germany sided with Austria, Russia’s alignment with Germany soon dissolved. Worsen the situation, they soon after allied with France, which put Germany in a bad position as they now have an opposing alliance on two fronts.
