Situation in Germany

The enthusiasm in the civil population was still very high due to the rapid victors of their army and more and more committed themselves to the army. The raw material supply was also good and the Germans didn't have to save anywhere. There have also been dozens of new jobs since the rise of the NSDAP, which sparked enthusiasm among the people.

Situation outside Germany

1939 is the year in which the Soviet Union and Germany invade Poland and start World War II. England and France declare war on Germany, but wait behind their own borders. The Poland campaign is going extremely well for Germany, a big contribution to this is the new "Blitzkrieg" tactic of the Germans. After Poland was defeated, the two winners split it up and Germany focused on France.
