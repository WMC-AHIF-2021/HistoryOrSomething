Medieval Times

In 1155, Frederick Barbarossa was elected Emperor, ending the rule of the Duke of Saxony and against the former emperors favoured heir. Before his election, there was a conflict between the Hohenstaufen and the so called, Welfs, but luckily, Frederick managed to make peace with them. The Hohenstaufen’s and a few emperors before attempted to reform the empire and also forced some of them through successfully.

Barbarossa

But Frederick especially wanted to emphasize the “Romanness” of the empire, partially through strengthening the emperor’s power and weaking the one of the pope, which became more and more powerful in the last decades. During the era of the Hohenstaufen, the german princes successfully and peacefully settled the eastern territories, which were uninhabited or sparsely populated by West Slavs. Also, many cities were founded like the future city on Munich or Freiburg.
