Ancient Greece

Today, the ancient Greeks are known for many things. These things are philosophy, mathematic and overall wisdom and knowledge. But unlike today, Greece wasn’T a unified state. It consisted of many smaller city states and was only unified in common culture and a general leadership, mostly Athens. Despite this, the greek states managed to influence many nations to this day on.

Begin of the Great Colonisation

In the year 750 b.c., the Greeks began establishing settlements all over the mediterranean. Some of these cities would later became important civilizations. This movement was the beginning of greek dominants all over europe and even the near east.
