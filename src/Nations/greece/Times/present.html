General Information

Greece is a developed country in southeastern Europe. It’s part of the Council of Europe, NATO, the EU, OECD, the WTO and the OSCE. As a naval nation with many islands, Greece possesses the most trading ships in the world. Greece is considered the root of western civilization, being the birthplace of democracy, theatre, Western philosophy and literature, major scientific and mathematical principles and of course, the Olympic Games. Greece is also a famous tourist attraction with more than 31 million visitors per year.

Important to Mention

In the last years, Greece experienced a huge dept crisis. Which had major consequences on its economy and government. More problems accured during the european migrant crisis in 2015, when a massive amount of refugees waited in Greece for access into the EU. Luckily for Greece, they managed to end the dept crisis in 2018.
