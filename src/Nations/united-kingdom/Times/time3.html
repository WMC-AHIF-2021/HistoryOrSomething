The Hundred Years War

Since the last century, the dynasty of the Angevin Empire has ended and led to the Hundred Years War between England and France, led by the House of Valois. Multiple battles have been fought throughout the years, and now it finally seems to come to and end. For the last hundred years, England fought for their remaining power on mainland europe but were ultimately defeated at the battle of Agincourt by Valois France and their allies.

A few years later, they would lose most of their land on mainland europe.
