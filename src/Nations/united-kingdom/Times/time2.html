Napoleon is defeated!

After Napoleon was defeated in Waterloo and France being exhausted from the past events, Britain remains the world's single superpower. After they lost most of their territories in america, due the 13 colonies revolting, the UK focused on the colonisation of India and Australia. They also occupied dutch indonesia while their core lands were occupied by the french in the coalition wars. Luckily for the Dutch, they, of course, gave them back. Most of them, though they kept the Cape of the good hope and bits of indonesia.

In the next decades, Britain dominates world trade and economy, even going so far to influence entire state economies like China and India.
