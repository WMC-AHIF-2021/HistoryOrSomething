The Great War's aftermatch

Four years ago, the Great War has ended and the central powers don't exist anymore. But it's not only them, who experienced a massive hit. Winner powers like France, Italy and even Britain were funded by the United States, to which they are now in debt. On the other hand, however, they managed to gain a lot of territory by more or less fair promises. They now have the Indian ocean connected to the mediterranean by a land route. Also, they occupied German colonies in africa and, of course, seized them after the war.

Irish War of Independence

Already during the last years of the First World War, Irish republicans launched multiple Risings against british rule until it reached its peak in 1919, when the Irish created a breakaway government and declared independence. One thing leads to another and war breaks out. The guerilla war against the Brits lasted 2 years, proved to be very successful for the Irish and resulted in the creation of the Republic of Ireland and Northern Ireland. However, the Republic would still remain a dominion of the British Empire.
