Spanish Empire

▹ Currency: Spanish real
▹ President: Charles IV
▹ Government: Absolute Monarchy
▹ Capital: Madrid
▹ Population: 24,5 Mio
▹ Area: 5,29 Million km²
Big but powerless
After the end of the Spanish Succession War (1701-1714), the rule of the Habsburgs in Spain was over and the House of Bourbon took over. It also marked the beginning of reform and decline for the Spanish Empire.

In 1788, Charles III died and his son Charles IV became king. His father, is considered "the most successful monarch of Spain after Ferdinand and Isabel", because of his intelligent and firm leadership. Historians say that Charles the III "rescued the Spaniards after waiting for a century".

Unfortunately, his son Charles IV is not really interested in ruling, but hunting. Because of this, most of the responsibility goes to his ministers and many of his decisions are influenced by his wife, Maria Luisa of Parma.

Source: Spanish Empire (1700-1810)
