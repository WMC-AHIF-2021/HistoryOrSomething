General Information

Spain is a developed country with King Felipe IV as its Monarch.
It possesses an advanced economy, has one of the longest life expectancies at 83.5 years. It is a world leader in organ transplants and organ donation.

Spain is part of many organisations, but the most important are the UN, NATO and of course the EU.

Most people will know Spain because of its widespread culture, language and of course football.
And this is one reason, why Spain is the 2nd most visited country in the world.

Important to Mention

In begin of the pandemic, Spain experienced a massive economic downturn such as many other
countries in europe. Adding that the current situation in the east with Russia and Ukraine affects all
of europe, Spain is no exception. Spain's foreign minister stated that Europe has to be "united" against the crisis.
