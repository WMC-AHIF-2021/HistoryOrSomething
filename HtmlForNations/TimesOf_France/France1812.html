Napoleonic France

Currency: the French franc Current Leader: Napoleon Bonaparte
Area: 1.200.000 km² Government: Monarchy (Dictatorship)
Population: 46 Mio. Capital: Paris

It's 1812, and France, under the leadership of Napoleon, has been at war
for the past 20 years and won every of the five coalitions.
France defeated many contributors such as Austria, Prussia and Sweden.
Furthermore, France created multiple Republics like the
Federation of the Rhine or a Republic in Switzerland.

Its economic blockade against Great Britain (1806) actually should weaken their enemy
and the island still felt the impact, but the UK avoided the biggest
economic downturn by simply increasing the trading with its colonies.
And to Napoleons disadvantage, the loyalty of his allies Spain and Russia began to crumble.
Spain was rebelling due to Napoleons intervention in the royal family and
Russia started trading with Britain in 1810, which lead to
Napoleons decision to invade the Russian Empire.
