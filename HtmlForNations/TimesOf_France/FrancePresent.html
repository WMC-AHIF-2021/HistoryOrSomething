France - Present

▹ Currency: Euro
▹ President: Emmanuel Macron
▹ Government: Constitutional Republic
▹ Capital: Paris
▹ Population: 67,39 Mio
▹ Area: 643.800 km²
Overall situation
In the modern day, France is still a influential nation as a founding member of various important organisations such as the EU or the UNO.
It's economy is in the top 10 and the military of the french is also top-class. With Britain out of the EU, France and Germany have become the major leaders of the Union. Because of historic reasons, many of its former colonies have french as an official language, making french one of the most spread languages.
France's Prime may be over and their status has started to fade, but it still plays an important role in the world.

Important to mention
As major user of nuclear power, France focused to legalise this energy source as "green". Of course, many opposed this for many years, but since January 2022, when France took over the presidency of the Council of the EU, they were able to get a step closer to achieve their goal. Also, the French, but especially their president, Emmanuel Macron, speak of the necessity for Europe's self-sufficiency, like the need for a new security order.
